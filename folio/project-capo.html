Our Capoeira

Capoeira’s origins and evolution are evidence that beautiful works of art often spring forth from periods of struggle, tragedy and despair.  Slaves, in Portuguese controlled colonial Brazil, used tribal music and deceptive movements to disguise their practice as a harmless dance.  They rooted this art in their new environment, an unknown and hostile land. Capoeira grew from the dark soil of their adversity to fuel their one hope of survival.
