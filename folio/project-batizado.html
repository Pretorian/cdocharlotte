The 2013 2nd International Capoeira Festival hosted by the Brazilian Arts Center in Charlotte, NC. was a great success.

Once upon a time, in Mestre Esquilo's academy...
As a representative of Grupo Cordao de Ouro, founded by the renowned Mestre Suassuna, Mestre Bruno (Esquilo) Melo opened the Brazilian Arts Center in 2013.
Since its opening, the BAC has welcomed many famous guests from the all over the world and the capoeira community.
The members of Cordao de Ouro Charlotte have come together as a capoeira family to promote the art form with the help of our community, always welcoming new members as they continue to contribute to the history of the capoeira movement.
This time we would like to thank you, our family, and extend our deepest feelings of appreciation to:
  • Mestre Suassuna
  • Mestre Accordeon
  • Mestre Panao
  • Mestre Doutor
  • Mestre Pinga Fogo
  • Mestre Kiki da Bahia
  • Contra Mestre Cebolinha
  • Contra Mestre Neginho
Each of these great personalities has contributed to the story of capoeira in Charlotte. We hope to share more days like this with you... until next time.. na volta do mundo... Axe!
Axé....!!
-Mestre Esquilo and the CDOCharlotte Family
